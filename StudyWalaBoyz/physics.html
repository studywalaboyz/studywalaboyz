Questions & Answers:

Q1: What is Physics?

Physics is a branch of science that studies matter, energy, and the relationship between them. It explains the laws of nature and helps in understanding the physical world.

Q2: What are the fundamental forces of nature?

The four fundamental forces are gravitational force, electromagnetic force, strong nuclear force, and weak nuclear force.
